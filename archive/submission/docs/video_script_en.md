# Team video script - English

Maximum duration: 3 minutes.

## Structure

1. Team and university identification.
2. Problem: autonomous racing on TORCS Corkscrew from standing start.
3. Technical approach: modular Python driver using sensors, speed planning, steering, traction and recovery.
4. AI/ML component: behavioral cloning experiment and why the final policy is hybrid.
5. IBM Granite and SkillsBuild: how the team used them for learning, review, tuning and communication.
6. Results: best lap, consistency, reliability and next improvements.
7. Closing: repository, blog/report and SkillsBuild call to action.

## Key sentence

Our goal was not only to produce a fast lap, but to build a stable, explainable and reproducible AI driver.
